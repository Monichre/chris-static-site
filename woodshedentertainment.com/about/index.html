About Woodshed Entertainment

Woodshed Entertainment began as an independent film production company with the 2009 Sundance hit, Brief Interviews with Hideous Men. Today, we produce everything from commercials, to branded content, narrative shorts, documentaries and feature films. In essence we are storytellers who can deliver any content to any platform.

Woodshed is comprised of three Director/Producers, Chris Ciancimino, Kevin Connors and Kat Westergaard. With years of experience and an extensive roster of collaborators in the independent film and commercial world, this core group has the ability to deliver compelling visual content for any budget.
